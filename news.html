CHS Spring Musical Package
Every year, Creekside High School's theatre department hosts the Spring Musical for our community to enjoy. Additionally, we told students about important dates and how to purchase tickets for the showing.

CHS Academy News Package
This news package informed rising freshmen and sophomores about the Career Academy programs at Creekside High School. Academies at Creekside are a great chance to get professional certifications and actual career experience.
